Welcome to Two Tasty Treats! My name is Porter, and I've created a website showcasing two of my favorite dishes, both a dinner and a dessert. Although it's only two items, they're some of the best things your oven will have the pleasure of having inside it! The dinner is a bunch of large pasta shells stuffed and surrounded by the alfredo sauce, chicken, bacon, and mozzarella and parmesan cheese. If you like chicken alfredo, you'll love this! The dessert is a very large sandwich. On top of a dessert being a sandwich, It's a sweet oatmeal cookie outside, with a super sweet chocolate inside, combining to make something that you don't want to eat if you have any cavities. The links to each of these recipes are right below this text. So try it out, and I hope you enjoy!
